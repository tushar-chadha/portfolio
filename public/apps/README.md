# App Icons

Add your app icons here:

1. Save your app icons in this folder:
   - `misfits.png` - Misfits Communities app icon
   - `peace.png` - Peace app icon

2. Then update the image sources in `app/page.tsx`:
   - Change `src="https://..."` to `src="/apps/misfits.png"`
   - Change the Peace app to `src="/apps/peace.png"`

## How to Get Your App Icons:

### From Play Store:
1. Go to your app's Play Store page
2. Right-click the app icon
3. Select "Save Image As..."
4. Save as PNG format

### From App Store Connect:
1. Download your app icon from App Store Connect
2. Or screenshot from the App Store listing
3. Save as PNG format

## Recommended Size:
- 512x512 pixels (or larger, will scale down)
- PNG format for transparency
